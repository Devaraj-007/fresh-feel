Frequently Asked Questions

How long do these pads take to biodegrade? +

Under proper composting conditions, our pads typically break down within 6-12 months. This is significantly faster than conventional pads which can take 500+ years to decompose.

Can I compost these pads at home? +

Yes, our pads are suitable for home composting. For best results, ensure your compost pile maintains proper temperature and moisture levels. The pads will break down completely, leaving no harmful residues.

Are the absorption capabilities comparable to regular pads? +

Absolutely! Our biodegradable pads use advanced bamboo fiber technology that provides excellent absorption while being eco-friendly. Many users report equal or better performance compared to conventional pads.

What makes these pads different from other 'eco-friendly' options? +

Unlike some products that are only partially biodegradable, our pads are 100% compostable. We also use herbal infusions for added health benefits and maintain complete transparency about our materials and manufacturing process.
