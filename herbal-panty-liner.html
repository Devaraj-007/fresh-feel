Panty Liners FAQs

How often should I change my panty liner? +

We recommend changing your panty liner every 3-4 hours or whenever it feels damp to maintain freshness and hygiene.

Can I use panty liners every day? +

Yes, our herbal panty liners are designed for daily use. The breathable materials and natural herbs make them suitable for everyday freshness.

Are these panty liners scented? +

No, our panty liners are fragrance-free. The fresh feeling comes from natural herbs like aloe vera and neem, not artificial perfumes.

How do I dispose of used panty liners? +

Wrap used liners in the provided wrapper or tissue and dispose of them in a waste bin. Do not flush them down the toilet.
