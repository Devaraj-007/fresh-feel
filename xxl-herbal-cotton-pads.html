Our XXL pads are larger than XL pads in both length and width, providing even more coverage and protection. They feature enhanced absorbency and additional herbal ingredients for maximum comfort during your heaviest flow days.

Our XXL pads include all the herbs from our XL pads (neem, aloe vera, turmeric) plus sandalwood, which is known for its exceptional cooling and soothing properties, providing extra comfort during heavy flow.
