Why Cotton Matters

Cotton is naturally breathable, reducing moisture buildup and the risk of bacterial growth. Unlike synthetic materials, cotton doesn't trap heat and allows your skin to breathe, preventing irritation and discomfort.

The Herbal Advantage

Our herbal infusion includes aloe vera for soothing, chamomile for anti-inflammatory benefits, and neem for its antibacterial properties. These natural ingredients work together to promote skin health during menstruation.

Yes, our cotton herbal pads are designed with a high-absorbency core that effectively manages flow. While they may feel different from synthetic pads initially, they provide reliable protection without the chemicals.

How do herbal infusions in pads work? +

The herbs are infused in a special layer that releases their beneficial properties when in contact with moisture. Aloe vera soothes, chamomile reduces inflammation, and neem provides antibacterial protection.

Absolutely! Cotton herbal pads are specifically designed for sensitive skin. The 100% organic cotton is hypoallergenic, and the absence of chemicals makes them ideal for those with skin sensitivities.

Unlike regular pads that contain plastic and take centuries to decompose, our cotton herbal pads use biodegradable materials. The production process is also more sustainable, with a lower environmental footprint.
