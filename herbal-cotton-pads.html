Cotton Pads FAQs

Are these cotton pads suitable for very sensitive skin? +

Yes, our pure cotton pads are specifically designed for sensitive skin. The 100% cotton surface is naturally hypoallergenic and free from synthetic materials that can cause irritation.

How do cotton pads compare to ultra-thin pads? +

While ultra-thin pads focus on discreetness, cotton pads prioritize natural comfort and breathability. Cotton pads are slightly thicker but offer superior softness and are ideal for those with sensitivity concerns.

Can I use cotton pads overnight? +

Absolutely! Our cotton pads are available in different absorbency levels, including XL and XXL options that are perfect for overnight use. The breathable design ensures comfort throughout the night.

Are the cotton pads biodegradable? +

Our cotton pads use predominantly natural materials that are more environmentally friendly than conventional pads. While not fully biodegradable, they contain significantly less plastic and are a more sustainable choice.
