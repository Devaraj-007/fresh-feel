How to Use

Apply Solution

Gently Wipe

Softly wipe the pad across your face in upward motions, avoiding the delicate eye area.

Allow the herbal infusion to fully absorb into your skin for maximum benefits before applying other products.

Yes, absolutely! Our herbal pads are specially formulated with gentle, natural ingredients that are perfect for sensitive skin types. They're free from harsh chemicals, alcohol, and artificial fragrances that can cause irritation.
