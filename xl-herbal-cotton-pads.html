XL Pads FAQs

How are XL pads different from regular pads? +

Our XL pads are 280mm in length compared to 225mm for regular pads. They feature an extended absorption core and additional leak-proof barriers on the sides and back for maximum protection during heavy flow days.

Can I use XL pads for overnight protection? +

Absolutely! The XL pads are specifically designed for overnight use. The extended length and superior absorption provide up to 8 hours of continuous protection while you sleep.

Are the herbs in XL pads different from regular pads? +

Yes, our XL pads contain an enhanced herbal blend with neem and turmeric in addition to chamomile and aloe vera. These additional herbs provide extra antibacterial properties beneficial for heavy flow days.

How often should I change an XL pad? +

While absorption varies by individual, our XL pads typically provide protection for 6-8 hours under normal heavy flow conditions. We recommend changing every 4-6 hours for optimal freshness and hygiene.
